# Lost & Found Festival 2026 — Complete Guide

**Dates:** Apr 23–27, 2026 | **Location:** Gianpula Village, Malta 🇲🇹 | **Capacity:** ~5,000 | **Stages:** 4

Five days of house and techno in Malta's iconic Gianpula Village outdoor venue complex. A spring festival in the Mediterranean sun — the perfect escape from UK winter hangover season.

---

## Overview

Lost & Found Festival takes place at Gianpula Village — a cluster of outdoor club spaces carved into the Maltese limestone on the outskirts of Valletta. April in Malta means 22–26°C daytime temperatures, clear skies, and warm evenings — genuinely ideal festival weather.

The lineup typically features Solomun, Amelie Lens, and leading house names. The festival is city-based (no camping) with Valletta and St Julian's as the main accommodation hubs.

## Getting There

**Nearest airport:** Malta International (MLA) — ~20 min
**Nearest city:** Valletta

Direct flights from London Gatwick, Heathrow, Manchester, Birmingham. The airport is on the south of the island — bus routes X4 and X1 connect to Valletta for €1.50. Official festival buses run from Valletta and St Julian's to Gianpula during the festival.
