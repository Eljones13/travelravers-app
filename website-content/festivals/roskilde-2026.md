# Roskilde Festival 2026 — Complete Guide

**Dates:** Jun 27 – Jul 4, 2026 | **Location:** Roskilde, Denmark 🇩🇰 | **Capacity:** ~130,000 | **Stages:** 8

Denmark's legendary non-profit festival — one of the largest and most respected music events in Europe. Running since 1971, Roskilde donates all profits to charity and consistently books the most eclectic, adventurous lineup on the continent.

---

## Overview

Roskilde takes place in Roskilde — a city 30km west of Copenhagen, connected by a 22-minute train ride from Copenhagen Central Station. The festival is camping-only and runs for 8 days, with music starting on Saturday and building to a peak across the final four days. The campsite opens days before the music — arriving early is part of the ritual.

## Getting There

**Nearest airport:** Copenhagen (CPH) — 22 min train to Roskilde
**Nearest city:** Copenhagen

Train from Copenhagen Central (København H) to Roskilde takes 22 minutes — one of the most convenient festival rail links in Europe. The Copenhagen Card covers unlimited train travel including the Roskilde line.
