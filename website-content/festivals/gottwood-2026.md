# Gottwood 2026 — Complete Guide

**Dates:** Jun 4–8, 2026 | **Location:** Anglesey, Wales, UK 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁷󠁬󠁳󠁿 | **Capacity:** ~1,500 | **Stages:** 5

Gottwood is one of the UK's most beloved small festivals — an intimate woodland gathering on Anglesey that punches far above its weight with consistently world-class bookings. 1,500 capacity, five stages, and a woodland setting that makes it unlike any other UK event.

---

## Overview

Gottwood occupies a private woodland on the Isle of Anglesey in North Wales. Unlike mainstream camping festivals, there are no big screens, no corporate sponsors, and no compromise on sound quality. The stages are clustered through the trees — finding each one is part of the experience. Artists like Call Super, Peggy Gou, Hunee, Move D, and Romare have all played.

The festival is camping-only and the site is accessed via a short shuttle from the designated car park. There are no hotels nearby — if you want comfort, stay in Bangor or Liverpool the night before and drive in.

## Getting There

**Nearest airport:** Manchester (MAN) — ~1.5 hours drive
**Nearest city:** Liverpool — ~1 hour drive

Drive across the Menai Strait bridge onto Anglesey. Festival car park is signposted from the A5. Shuttle buses run to the site from the car park. No public transport to the site.
