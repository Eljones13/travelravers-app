# Love International 2026 — Complete Guide

**Dates:** Jul 8–15, 2026 | **Location:** The Garden, Tisno, Croatia 🇭🇷 | **Capacity:** ~3,000 | **Stages:** 3

A week-long celebration of disco, house, soul, and electronic music at The Garden resort on the Croatian Adriatic coast. Intimate, warm, and consistently one of the best-curated lineups in Europe.

---

## Overview

Love International takes place at The Garden resort in Tisno — the same stunning venue that hosts Defected Croatia. A week of music across floating stages on the sea, an outdoor terrace stage, and a forest stage, with headline sets from artists like Floating Points, Honey Dijon, DJ Koze, Nora En Pure, and Move D.

The festival is non-camping — most attendees stay in on-site apartments at The Garden resort or in private accommodation in Tisno village. The relaxed Mediterranean pace is part of the appeal: days are for swimming and boat trips, evenings are for dancing.

## Getting There

**Nearest airport:** Split (SPU) — ~45 min drive
**Nearest city:** Split

Fly into Split Airport from London (Gatwick, Heathrow, Stansted), Manchester, or Leeds Bradford. The Garden resort offers official transfers from Split Airport during festival week — check loveinternational.hr for packages.
