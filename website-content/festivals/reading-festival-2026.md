# Reading Festival 2026 — Complete Guide

**Dates:** Aug 22–24, 2026 | **Location:** Richfield Avenue, Reading, UK 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿 | **Capacity:** ~105,000 | **Stages:** 6

The UK's most iconic camping festival, running since 1971 at Richfield Avenue beside the River Thames. Shared lineup with Leeds Festival across six stages of rock, pop, alternative and electronic music.

---

## Overview

Reading Festival takes place at Richfield Avenue — a 20-minute walk from Reading train station along the Thames towpath. It's one of the most accessible major UK festivals by public transport: direct trains from London Paddington take 25 minutes.

## Getting There

**Nearest airport:** London Heathrow (LHR) — ~45 min
**Nearest city:** Reading

Train to Reading station from London Paddington (25 min, ~£15). Walk 20 minutes along the Thames or take a short shuttle bus to the site. One of the easiest major UK festivals to reach by train.

## Where to Stay

On-site camping at Richfield Avenue is the standard experience. Premier Inn and Ibis Reading are both within walking distance for non-campers.
