# Sea Dance Festival 2026 — Complete Guide

**Dates:** Aug 27–30, 2026 | **Location:** Buljarica Beach, Petrovac, Montenegro 🇲🇪 | **Capacity:** ~20,000 | **Stages:** 3

Four nights of electronic music on a stunning Adriatic beach in Montenegro. Sea Dance is the region's answer to Croatia's festival scene — equally beautiful coastline, smaller crowds, and often stronger lineups than you'd expect.

---

## Overview

Sea Dance Festival takes place on Buljarica Beach near Petrovac on the southern Montenegrin coast. The beach stage is set up directly on the sand with the Adriatic as a backdrop — one of the most visually stunning festival settings in Europe. Camping is available on-site.

Artists like Disclosure, Fisher, Solomun, and DJ Snake have headlined in recent years.

## Getting There

**Nearest airport:** Tivat (TIV) — ~45 min
**Nearest city:** Budva

Fly into Tivat Airport (easyJet from Gatwick, Ryanair from various UK airports in summer). Budva is the nearest resort town — a 15-minute drive from the festival site. Pre-book transfers or rent a car.

## Where to Stay

Budva is the best base — 15km from the festival with a buzzing strip of bars, restaurants and beach clubs. Petrovac village has guesthouses within walking distance of the site.
