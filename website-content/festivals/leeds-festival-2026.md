# Leeds Festival 2026 — Complete Guide

**Dates:** Aug 22–24, 2026 | **Location:** Bramham Park, Leeds, UK 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿 | **Capacity:** ~75,000 | **Stages:** 6

One of the UK's biggest and most iconic camping festivals, running in parallel with Reading Festival across the same weekend every August Bank Holiday. Rock, pop, electronic, and alternative across six stages.

---

## Overview

Leeds Festival takes place at Bramham Park in West Yorkshire, around 10 miles south of Leeds city centre. It's a full camping experience with multiple ticket tiers (General Admission camping through to boutique glamping options). The lineup mirrors Reading Festival exactly, with headliners split across the main stage on Friday, Saturday and Sunday nights.

## Getting There

**Nearest airport:** Leeds Bradford (LBA) — ~20 min drive
**Nearest city:** Leeds

Train to Leeds City Station — official Leeds Festival shuttle buses run directly to Bramham Park all weekend. Book shuttles in advance as they sell out.
