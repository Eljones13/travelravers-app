# Melt Festival 2026 — Complete Guide

**Dates:** Jul 17–19, 2026 | **Location:** Ferropolis, Gräfenhainichen, Germany 🇩🇪 | **Capacity:** ~25,000 | **Stages:** 3

Three days of electronic music, techno, and indie at one of Europe's most spectacular festival venues — Ferropolis, the "City of Iron", an open-air museum of giant mining excavators in former East Germany.

---

## Overview

Ferropolis is unlike anywhere else in music — a peninsula of five giant bucket-wheel excavators rising from a lake in the Saxony-Anhalt coal fields. Melt stages its main stage between these machines, with sets carrying across the water at night. The combination of industrial architecture and electronic music is genuinely awe-inspiring.

Artists like Bicep, Four Tet, Floating Points and Peggy Gou have all headlined. The festival is camping-only.

## Getting There

**Nearest airport:** Berlin Brandenburg (BER) — ~1 hour
**Nearest city:** Berlin

ICE trains from Berlin Hauptbahnhof to Dessau (50 min), then official Melt shuttle buses from Dessau station to Ferropolis. Buy your DB train ticket in advance.

**[Search flights to Berlin BER →](https://www.skyscanner.net/transport/flights-from/ber/)**

## Where to Stay

Camping at Ferropolis is the only option and the right choice — sleeping between the excavators is unmissable. Berlin makes an excellent pre/post festival base.
