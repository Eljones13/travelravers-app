# Time Warp 2026 — Complete Guide

**Dates:** Mar 28–29, 2026 | **Location:** Rosengarten, Mannheim, Germany 🇩🇪 | **Capacity:** ~10,000 | **Stages:** 4

One of the world's premier techno events — two nights of uncompromising techno and house in Mannheim's Rosengarten convention centre. Running since 1994, Time Warp maintains a reputation for impeccable sound and bookings.

---

## Overview

Time Warp is a club event, not a camping festival. Held over two nights in the Rosengarten Mannheim — a well-designed convention venue that keeps the sound quality high across its four rooms. Artists like Richie Hawtin, Adam Beyer, Charlotte de Witte, and Amelie Lens regularly headline.

## Getting There

**Nearest airport:** Frankfurt (FRA) — ~1 hour
**Nearest city:** Mannheim

ICE train from Frankfurt Hauptbahnhof to Mannheim Hauptbahnhof takes ~40 minutes. The Rosengarten venue is a 10-minute walk from the station.

## What to Pack

March in Mannheim is cold (5–12°C) — this is a club event, not an outdoor festival.

- Warm coat for the walk to/from venue
- Earplugs (high-quality — Time Warp's sound is loud)
