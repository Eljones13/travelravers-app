# Croatia Festival Travel Guide 2026: Defected, Love International & Hideout

Croatia is the raver's paradise: stunning Adriatic coast, cheap flights from the UK, and some of the best house and techno festivals in Europe all clustered around Tisno and Pag Island. Here's how to do it properly.

---

## The Festivals

### ⚠️ Defected Croatia — FINAL EVER EDITION

**Dates:** Aug 12–16, 2026 | **Location:** The Garden, Tisno | **Tickets:** [Skiddle →](https://www.skiddle.com/festivals/defected-croatia/?sktag=15628)

House music royalty at the Garden resort on the Adriatic. Multiple floating stages, day parties, boat parties, and some of the best tech house and deep house bookings in Europe. 2026 is the final ever edition of Defected Croatia — this is a once-in-a-lifetime ticket. Don't miss it.

### Love International

**Dates:** Jul 8–15, 2026 | **Location:** The Garden, Tisno | **Tickets:** [Official →](https://loveinternational.hr/tickets)

A week-long celebration of disco, house, soul, and electronic music at the same Garden resort. Smaller and more intimate than Defected, Love International has a devoted following and a notoriously warm atmosphere. Floating stages, outdoor terraces, and sunset sets from world-class DJs.

### Hideout

**Dates:** Jun 30 – Jul 4, 2026 | **Location:** Zrće Beach, Pag Island

Five days of drum and bass, house and techno at Zrće Beach — one of Europe's most famous party beaches. Three outdoor venues, 24-hour music, and the Croatian summer sun. A bucket-list experience for UK ravers.

## Getting There: Fly into Split SPU

**Fly into Split Airport (SPU)** — the main hub for both Tisno and Pag Island. Ryanair, easyJet and Jet2 all fly direct from London, Manchester, Leeds and Bristol.

**[Search flights to Split (SPU) on Skyscanner →](https://www.skyscanner.net/transport/flights-from/spu/)**

From Split, **hire a car** — the drive to Tisno takes about 45 minutes along the coastal road. For Pag Island, allow 1.5–2 hours. Car hire at Split is cheap in the off-season but expensive during peak festival weeks. Book months ahead.

## Essential Croatia Gear (Heat Survival Kit)

Croatia in July and August means 32–38°C temperatures, direct sun on exposed venues, and sea spray on boat parties. Pack for the heat.

Non-negotiable for Adriatic coast festivals. Apply before you get dressed, reapply every 2 hours. Reef-safe is essential if you're swimming.
