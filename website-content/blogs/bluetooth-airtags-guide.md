# Best Bluetooth Trackers & AirTags for Festivals 2026: Never Lose Your Tent Again

Festival theft is more common than people admit. Tents get ransacked during headliners, bags go missing in campsites, and cars get broken into in festival car parks. A £25–30 Bluetooth tracker is cheap insurance against all three.

---

## Why You Need a Festival Tracker

At a 70,000-person festival, your tent is one of 20,000 in a field. Without a landmark or tracker, finding it after midnight in the rain is genuinely difficult. Beyond navigation, theft is a real problem at UK camping festivals — most tent break-ins happen during Saturday headliners when the campsite empties.

A tracker won't prevent theft, but it significantly increases the chance of recovering stolen items and helps you navigate back to your pitch in the dark.
