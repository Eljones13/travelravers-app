## Defected Croatia 2026 — "One Last Dance"

Dates: 29 July – 2 August 2026. Venue: The Garden Resort, Ul. Petrića Glava 34, 22240 Tisno, Croatia. GPS: 43.8068°N, 15.6404°E. Capacity: ~5,000. Age: 18+. Cashless RFID. 95% SOLD OUT.

Genres: House, deep house, vocal house, nu-disco, soulful house, garage, afro house, disco.
Energy: 7/10 | Darkness: 2/10 | First-Timer: 8/10

### Survival Intel
1. Sun exceeds 35°C in late July — SPF, hat, and electrolytes essential
2. Book Barbarella's tickets in advance — they sell independently and go fast; shuttle ~€4pp
3. Bring reef shoes — sea urchins on rocks around swimming/dancing areas
4. Croatia uses Euro; RFID cashless on-site but keep cash for taxis
5. Carry valid passport (Croatia is Schengen); GHIC/EHIC for health coverage

### Travel
Fly London → Zadar (ZAD, ~2.5h, £40–80 one-way). Official coach transfer ~£30 each way. Total ~5–7 hours.

### Top 10 Artists
Groove Armada, Moodymann, Jayda G, Seth Troxler, Carl Craig, Purple Disco Machine, TSHA, Floorplan, Skream, Dennis Ferrer

## Love International 2026

Dates: Wednesday 8 July – Tuesday 14 July 2026 (7 days). Venue: The Garden Resort, Tisno, Croatia. GPS: 43.8068°N, 15.6404°E. Capacity: ~5,000. Age: 18+. Cashless RFID (€3.50 activation). Pass: £195+BF.

Genres: Deep house, techno, minimal, ambient, Balearic, disco, leftfield electronica, breaks, dub, Italian house, progressive house.
Energy: 6/10 | Darkness: 5/10 | First-Timer: 6/10

### Survival Intel
1. Pace yourself — 7 days of 4-stage programming, boat parties, and Barbarella's causes burnout
2. Carry ID at all times — Croatian law requires it; €400 on-the-spot fines reported
3. Reef shoes essential — sea urchins endemic to the rocky Adriatic coastline
4. Book Barbarella's separately and early — sells out; shuttle nightly
5. Free water refills on-site (near shower block); no outside food/drink permitted

### Travel
Fly London → Zadar (ZAD, ~2.5h, £40–80 one-way). Official coach ~£30 each way. Total ~5–7 hours.

### Top 10 Artists
Daniel Avery, Midland, Nightmares on Wax (DJ), Call Super, Saoirse, Palms Trax, Craig Richards, Mathew Jonson (Live), Shanti Celeste, Optimo (Espacio)

## Dimensions Festival 2026

Dates: 27–31 August 2026. Venue: The Garden Resort + Barbarella's, Tisno, Croatia. GPS: 43.8068°N, 15.6404°E. Capacity: ~5,000. Age: 18+ (Challenge 25). Cashless RFID. Sold via RA only. ~£190+BF.

Genres: Techno, electro, minimal, ambient, breaks, acid, deep house, experimental electronics, dub.
Energy: 7/10 | Darkness: 7/10 | First-Timer: 5/10

### Survival Intel
1. On-site lockers ~€8/day + €50 RFID deposit (refunded on return)
2. Zero-tolerance drug policy — Croatian police, minimum £300 fine, bag searches on entry
3. Boat parties require separate tickets; arrive at jetty 30 min early, no refunds for late arrivals
4. Bring empty reusable water bottle; ATMs in Tisno can run dry — withdraw cash before arriving
5. Medical/welfare tent on-site; nearest hospital Šibenik (20km); carry GHIC/EHIC + travel insurance

### Travel
Fly London → Zadar (ZAD) or Split (SPU), ~2.5h, £40–80 one-way. Official coach ~£30 each way. Total ~5–7 hours.

### Top 10 Artists
Helena Hauff b2b Moopie, John Talabot, Satoshi Tomiie (Live), Sonja Moonear, Eris Drew & Octo Octa, Marcellus Pittman, Objekt, Moritz Von Oswald, Roza Terenzi (Live), DMX Krew (Live)

## Electric Love Festival 2026

Dates: 9–11 July 2026 (Thu–Sat); warm-up Wed 8 July. Venue: Salzburgring, Plainfeld near Salzburg, Austria. GPS: 47.7437°N, 13.1779°E. Capacity: ~180,000. Age: 16+ (VIP 18+). Cashless digital wristband. Festival Pass Phase 3 €219. Camping €50.

Genres: EDM, progressive house, big room, trance, hardstyle, hard dance, drum & bass, future bass, melodic techno.
Energy: 9/10 | Darkness: 3/10 | First-Timer: 8/10

### Survival Intel
1. Thunderstorms are almost guaranteed — invest in a waterproof tent; cheap pop-ups will not survive
2. Campsite has zero shade — sun heats tents by 8am; bring reflective tarp; earplugs essential
3. Lock everything — theft reported at campsite; use padlocks and lockers
4. €15 garbage bag deposit refunded when returned full — clever system, keep yours
5. Skip the Wednesday warm-up or lower expectations — the main days (Thu–Sat) are significantly better

### Travel
Fly London → Salzburg (SZG, ~2h, £40–80 one-way). Bus 2 or 27 to Salzburg Central Station. FREE Electric Love shuttle to festival. Total ~4–5 hours.

### Top 10 Artists
Swedish House Mafia, Armin van Buuren, Paul Kalkbrenner, Pendulum (DJ Set), Dimitri Vegas, KSHMR, John Newman, Hugel, BUNT., Annix

## Shared Tisno Notes (Defected, Love International, Dimensions)
- Same venue: The Garden Resort, Tisno
- Airports: Zadar (ZAD) 40 min or Split (SPU) 60 min
- All festivals: official coach transfers ~£30 each way
- Uber doesn't operate; garden taxis wait outside site; night rates 3–5x daytime
- Barbarella's (Pirovac): legendary afterparty venue, shuttle ~€4pp or speedboat taxi
- Hotel Miran: official partner hotel for all three, 12 min from site, 2 pools, private beach
- Reef shoes mandatory; temperature regularly 35°C+; carry Euro cash; GHIC essential
