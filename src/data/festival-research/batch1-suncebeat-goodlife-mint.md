Energy: 7/10 | Darkness: 2/10 | First-Timer: 9/10

### Survival Intel
1. Book boat party tickets early — they sell out fast
2. Stay in Costa da Caparica or Lisbon — no on-site accommodation
3. Pack SPF 50+ suncream and a hat; Atlantic sun is intense
4. Night buses run until 4am back to Lisbon (Alcântara, Cais do Sodré)
5. Bar staff have short-changed customers — check your change

### Travel

## 2. GOODLIFE FESTIVAL 2026

### Basic Info
Date: Saturday 6 June 2026, 12pm–11pm. Venue: Harewood House, Leeds LS17 9LG. Capacity: ~12,000. Age: 18+. No camping.

Genres: Classic house, vocal house, soulful house, disco, nu-disco, funk, soul.
Energy: 7/10 | Darkness: 1/10 | First-Timer: 9/10

### Survival Intel
1. No food/drink allowed in — budget for on-site food and drinks
2. Bring a blanket — camping chairs not permitted
3. No pass-outs — once you leave you cannot re-enter
4. Arrive early — last entry 4:30pm; queues reported at debut 2025 event
5. Bag max 35cm x 40cm x 19cm; no aerosols; roll-on deodorant only

### Travel
Train: London Kings Cross → Leeds (2h 10m LNER). Bus No.36 from Leeds to Harewood (20 mins). Total ~2.5–3 hours.

### Top 10 Artists
Armand Van Helden, Groove Armada, Roger Sanchez, Inner City (Live), The Shapeshifters, Craig Charles, Alison Limerick, K-Klass, Michael Gray, Ian Ossia

## 3. MINT FESTIVAL 2026

### Basic Info
Dates: Sat 2 May & Sun 3 May 2026. Venue: Newsam Green Farm, Leeds LS15 9AD. Capacity: ~7,500–10,000/day. Age: 18+. No camping.

Genres: Tech house, minimal house, deep tech, house, techno, UK garage, bassline.
Energy: 8/10 | Darkness: 5/10 | First-Timer: 6/10

### Survival Intel
1. Get to shuttle bus early — return queues can be very long
2. Arrive early — entry queues have been an hour+ in past years
3. Sign up on mintfestival.co.uk for ticket access — sells out instantly
4. Free return shuttle bus included if tickets bought before 31 December
5. No food or drink allowed in — budget for on-site spending

### Travel
Train: London Kings Cross → Leeds (2h 10m). Free shuttle bus from Leeds city centre (if ticket bought before 31 Dec). Total ~2.5–3 hours.

### Top 10 Artists
Enzo Siragusa, Ewan McVicar, Luuk Van Dijk, Max Dean, Shanti Celeste, Sidney Charles, Obskur, Danny Bond, Morgan Seatree, Sally C
